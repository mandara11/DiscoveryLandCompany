is a US-based real estate developer and operator of private

The Discovery Story

For almost three decades, Discovery has built communities that encourage both our
development teams and members themselves to dream up the most fun experiences
imaginable. The first development in Scottsdale set the standard for a Discovery
community, complete with a championship golf course, stocked-to-the-brim comfort
stations, luxurious residential offerings, five-star service, and more; since then, each
community has built on the fun from the one that came before.
